Overview


Background

Over the last decade, the construction industry has seen a rapid rise, particularly in the mid-range and premium slabs of urban housing. Land being the prime input to housing development has led to aggressive investments through vast purchasing of land. However, with the current recessionary cycle and currency demonetization, land hoarders have realized that, investment in land with the intention of development, would bear greater fruits rather than holding it for future resale.

Our Goals

At MakeMeBuilder we help builders finding resources for visualizing, designing, creating, constructing and post construction operations. We help progressive landowners aspiring to become builders to embark on an entrepreneurial journey and self-develop and manage projects. Through our online marketplace (under development) we can help anyone find specific services and solutions for construction. For those who are starting raw we can set up complete team with outsourced talent and solution.

The Oppurtunity

To realize the full potential of private land holdings and expose them to development, it is imperative that assistance and step by step guidance be available to aspiring developers. The government’s policies for easing provisions and delivering homes with basic amenities to millions of first-time home owners has supported this segment of the market. We will focus on lands with potential of creating very large, untapped segment of affordable homes in emerging areas along with basic amenities. Old buildings in high density locations can also benefit from redevelopment.

Our Ambition

Make Me Builder aspires to be known as a leading incubator, knowledge and resource center for innovation in construction technology and project management. All these efforts will collectively lead to creation of successful companies in core sectors of the economy in alignment with government’s vision and policies. MakeMeBuilder wants be instrumental in developing sustainable and affordable projects in mixed-use housing, education, healthcare, retail, warehousing and urban infrastructure.
